Commercial Projects

We have good experience and success helping companies get started with fabric projects in fashion, health, and safety apparel areas. We can work with you on product design, and prototyping, to turn your creative idea into a product that can be efficiently manufactured. We do low volume production for samples and early customers. When your design starts to take off, we will construct accurate patterns and a manufacturing video so you can transfer higher volume production to an offshore resource.

Example: High end origami bow ties, featured at the 2015 Golden Globes.
Example: Dust, Smoke and Pollen Breathing Masks with fun patterns.
Example: Innovative style high end neck ties with designer fabrics.
